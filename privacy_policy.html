We collect various types of information when you use our website, including:

  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your experience on our site.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process orders and provide services.
  • Improve user experience on our website.
  • Send promotional offers and updates (optional).

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Sharing Your Information

Your personal information will not be shared with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • To process payments.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect certain features of our website.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please check this page regularly for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at support@ezybuy.com.
